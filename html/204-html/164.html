DYAP 88.3 is a music FM station owned by Southern Broadcasting Network and Solar Television Network. The station's studio and transmitter is located at San Carlos Heights, Quiot Pardo, Cebu City. DYAP is operates from Mondays 'til Sundays, 12:00nn-8:00pm only.

History

  • First broadcast as DyAP FM 88.3.
  • Then First FM 88.3 with an all adult contemporary music.
  • Since late 2003, it became Mom's Radio 88.3. Dedicated to the mothers and mother-to-be listeners in Cebu City.
  • In 2010, it reverted as DYAP 88.3.
