The 1999 South Asian Games (or 8th SAF Games) were held in Kathmandu, Nepal from September 25 to October 4, 1999.

Medal tally

 Rank  Nation Gold Silver Bronze Total
1  India 102 58 37 197
2    Nepal 32 10 24 65
3  Sri Lanka 16 42 62 120
4  Pakistan 10 36 30 76
5  Bangladesh 2 10 35 47
6  Bhutan 1 6 7 14
7  Maldives 0 0 4 4

Note: 28 Golds of Nepal came from Taekwondo and Karate.[1] The gold medal won by Pramila Thapa in taekwondo was the first gold medal in Nepal's taekwondo history and sports council's[clarification needed] history.
