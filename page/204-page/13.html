123rd Kentucky Derby
Kentucky Derby
Location Churchill Downs
Date 3 May 1997
Winning horse Silver Charm
Jockey Gary L. Stevens

The 1997 Kentucky Derby was the 123rd running of the Kentucky Derby. The race took place on May 3, 1997. There were 141,000 in attendance.[1]
